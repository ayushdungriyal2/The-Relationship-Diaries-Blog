How can I make him feel special in a long-distance relationship?

It can be difficult to feel close to your partner when you are in a long-distance relationship. However, there are things you can do to make them feel special and loved. One way is to send them thoughtful gifts. You can send them something that reminds you of them or that they've been wanting. Another way is to write them love letters. This is a great way to express your feelings and let them know how much you care about them. You can also send them photos or videos of yourself so they can see your face and hear your voice. Another way to stay connected is to schedule regular video chats or phone calls. This will help you feel close to each other and catch up on what's going on in each other's lives. You can also surprise your partner with little things like sending a care package or planning a trip to visit each other. It's important to communicate with each other and let each other know what you are thinking and feeling. This will help you both feel closer to each other even though you are apart. There are many ways to make someone feel special, even when you are not together in person. It just takes a little creativity and effort

Who is more likely to break up in a relationship?

There's no easy answer to this question, as there are many factors that can contribute to a relationship break-up. This can often lead to unrealistic expectations and disappointment down the road. Another group is those who have been in a long-term relationship and have grown apart over time. How long do most online relationships last?

It's hard to say how long most online relationships last, because there are so many variables. For example, some relationships may only last a few weeks or months, while others may last for years. It also depends on the type of relationship you are in. For example, a casual relationship might not last as long as a more committed one. There are a few things that can affect how long an online relationship lasts. The first is the level of commitment. If both people are only interested in a casual relationship, it's less likely to last for a long time. However, if both people are looking for something more serious, the relationship is more likely to last longer. Another factor is communication. If both people are good at communicating with each other, it's more likely that the relationship will last longer. However, if communication is poor, it can lead to misunderstandings and conflict, which can cause the relationship to end prematurely. Finally, there's the issue of trust. If both people trust each other and feel comfortable sharing personal information with each other, it's more likely that the relationship will last longer. However, if there's no trust between the two people, it's more likely that the relationship will end sooner rather than later. These are just some of the factors that can affect how long an online relationship lasts. Ultimately, it depends on the two people involved and what they're looking for in the relationship
