What is the most difficult stage of marriage?

The most difficult stage of marriage is often the first year. This is when couples are adjusting to married life and learning how to live with each other. There are a lot of changes that take place during this time, and it can be tough to adjust. After the first year, things usually start to settle down and become more routine. However, there are still challenges that come up from time to time. The key is to communication and work together as a team. If you can do this, you'll be able to overcome anything that comes your way.

When to call it quits in a relationship?

There are a lot of different factors that can contribute to the decision to call it quits in a relationship. It might be because of infidelity, financial problems, or simply because the two people are no longer compatible. Sometimes, people stay in relationships for far too long and end up getting hurt as a result. If you are considering calling it quits in your relationship, there are a few things you should ask yourself first. The first is whether or not you are truly happy. If you are constantly fighting with your partner or feeling unfulfilled, it might be time to move on. The second is whether or not you see a future with this person. If you don't see yourself being with this person in the long-term, it's probably not worth staying in the relationship. The third is whether or not your relationship is having a negative impact on your life. If your relationship is causing more stress than happiness, it's probably time to call it quits. These are just a few things to consider before making the decision to call it quits in your relationship. Ultimately, only you can decide what's best for you.

How it feels to be a second wife?

It's not easy being a second wife. You are constantly compared to the first wife and you will never be able to measure up. You are also always aware of the fact that you are not the first choice. The first wife is always going to be special and you will always be second best. It's a hard position to be in, but it's also one that comes with its own set of perks.
