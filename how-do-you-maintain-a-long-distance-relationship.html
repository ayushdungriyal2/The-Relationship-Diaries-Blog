How do you maintain a long-distance relationship?

It can be difficult to maintain a long-distance relationship, but there are a few things you can do to make it work. The first is to communicate regularly. This means making time for regular phone calls or video chats. You should also try to visit each other in person as often as possible. Even if you can't visit often, you can still stay in touch by sending letters or emails. Another important thing to remember is to respect each other's space and independence. Just because you are in a long-distance relationship does not mean you have to be attached at the hip. It's important to give each other some time and space to pursue your own interests and hobbies. Lastly, it's important to trust each other. This can be difficult when you are not able to see each other every day, but it's essential for any relationship, long-distance or otherwise. If you follow these tips, you should be able to maintain a healthy and happy long-distance relationship
