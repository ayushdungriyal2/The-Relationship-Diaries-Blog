What happens when a man falls in love?

When a man falls in love, he experiences a range of emotions that can be both overwhelming and confusing. He may feel elated one minute and anxious the next. He may find himself daydreaming about the object of his affection and obsessively thinking about her when she is not around. He may also have trouble sleeping and concentrating on other things. The intensity of these emotions can be both exhilarating and exhausting. The man may also start to behave differently around the woman he loves. He may become more protective of her, wanting to spend as much time with her as possible. He may also start to do things that he thinks will please her, even if they're not necessarily things that he enjoys doing himself. Ultimately, falling in love is a very personal experience that differs from person to person. There is no right or wrong way to feel when you are in love; it's simply a matter of following your heart and seeing where it takes you.

What does dating look like?
