What age do most divorces happen?

There is no one answer to this question as the age at which divorces happen can vary greatly. However, there are some general trends that can be observed. For example, it is generally agreed that the majority of divorces happen among couples who are in their early to mid-40s. This is often attributed to the fact that this is the age when many couples have been married for a significant amount of time and may have started to experience some of the challenges that come with long-term relationships. Additionally, this is often also the age at which people are starting to think about retirement and may be facing other major life transitions, such as empty nest syndrome. These factors can all contribute to a higher rate of divorce among this age group. It is also worth noting that there has been a recent trend of people getting divorced later in life.
