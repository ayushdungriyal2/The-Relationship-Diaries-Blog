What are the signs that a relationship is over?

There are a few signs that a relationship is over. One of the most obvious signs is when communication starts to break down. This can happen in a number of ways, but it usually means that you and your partner are no longer talking about the things that are important to you. Another sign is when you start to notice that you and your partner are growing apart. This can be because you are no longer interested in the same things, or because you are no longer spending time together. It can also be because there's been a major change in one of your lives, such as a new job or moving to a new city. Lastly, another sign that a relationship is over is when one or both partners start to feel like they're not being fulfilled by the relationship. This can be because of different expectations, or because one person feels like they're not getting what they need out of the relationship. If you are noticing any of these signs, it's important to talk to your partner about them. It's possible that the relationship can be saved, but it's also possible that it's time to move on.
