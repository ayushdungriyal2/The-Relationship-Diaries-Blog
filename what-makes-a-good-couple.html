What makes a good couple?

A good couple is one that can communicate effectively, compromise when necessary, and respect each other's boundaries. They should also be able to have fun together and be supportive of each other's goals. A good couple is one that makes each other feel loved and special. They should be able to express their love for each other in both words and actions. A good couple is one that is willing to work on their relationship when things are tough. They should be able to seek help from a therapist or counselor when needed. A good couple is one that knows how to apologize and forgive each other. They should also be willing to accept responsibility for their own actions. A good couple is one that has a strong foundation of trust. They should be able to rely on each other for both the big and small things in life.
