What kills long-distance relationships?

There are a number of things that can kill a long-distance relationship. The first is simply the distance itself. It can be hard to maintain a connection with someone when you are not able to see them regularly. The second is communication. It's important to be able to communicate effectively with your partner, especially when you are not able to see each other in person. If you are not communicating well, it can lead to misunderstandings and resentment. The third is trust. It can be hard to trust your partner when you are not able to see them on a regular basis. If there's no trust, it can lead to jealousy and insecurity. Finally, time management is important in any relationship, but it's especially important in a long-distance relationship. If you are not able to manage your time well, it can lead to frustration and resentment on both sides. There are a number of things that can kill a long-distance relationship, but the most important thing is communication.
