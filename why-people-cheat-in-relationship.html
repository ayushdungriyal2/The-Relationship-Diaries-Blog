How often should I text in a long-distance relationship?

When you are in a long-distance relationship, it can be hard to know how often to text your partner. You don't want to seem clingy, but you also don't want to seem like you don't care. It's important to find a balance that works for both of you. A good rule of thumb is to text your partner every day, but not too much. You don't need to blow up their phone with texts, but a quick "Good morning" or "I love you" text can go a long way. You should also try to have at least one longer conversation each week. This can be done over the phone, video chat, or even just by sending each other long emails. It's important to keep the communication lines open in a long-distance relationship, so don't be afraid to reach out and touch base with your partner regularly.
