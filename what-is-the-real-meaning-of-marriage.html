What is the real meaning of marriage?

The real meaning of marriage is a lifelong commitment between two people who love each other. Marriage is more than just a legal contract; it is a promise to love and cherish each other for better or for worse. Marriage is a partnership where both husband and wife share the responsibilities of running the household and raising the children. In a good marriage, both partners are equal partners who support and care for each other. A successful marriage requires communication, compromise, patience, and understanding. It's not always easy, but it's worth it. The real meaning of marriage is that it is a sacred bond between two people who have pledged their hearts to each other. It is a symbol of their everlasting love and commitment.

Another sign that a marriage may be headed for divorce is when there's little or no intimacy between the spouses. Intimacy includes both physical and emotional closeness.
