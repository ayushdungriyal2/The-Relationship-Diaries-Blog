What do you call a relationship before dating?

What happens when a man falls in love?

When a man falls in love, he experiences a range of emotions that can be both exhilarating and overwhelming. He may feel happy, excited, nervous, anxious, and even scared all at the same time. The experience of love can be so powerful that it can cause a man to change his behavior in significant ways. He may find himself wanting to spend more time with her, doing things he wouldn't normally do just to make her happy, and making sacrifices for her sake. While the experience of love can be wonderful, it can also be scary. A man who is in love may worry about losing the woman he loves or about being rejected by her.
