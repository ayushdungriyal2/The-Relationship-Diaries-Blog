What is casual dating?

Casual dating is a type of relationship where the people involved do not have any expectations of each other, are not committed to each other, and are not exclusive. Casual dating is often referred to as a "no strings attached" relationship. With casual dating, there is no need for emotional attachment or commitment. This type of relationship can be beneficial because it allows you to explore your options and meet new people without getting too emotionally attached. However, casual dating can also be difficult because it can be hard to find someone who is interested in the same things as you are. It is also easy to get hurt emotionally in a casual relationship if you start to develop feelings for the other person but they do not feel the same way about you. If you are considering entering into a casual relationship, it is important to communicate your expectations and boundaries with the other person. It is also important to make sure that both you and the other person are on the same page in terms of what you want from the relationship. Otherwise, there could be some misunderstandings down the road.

What does dating mean to a guy?

Dating to a guy can mean a few different things. It can mean going out on dates with multiple people, trying to find someone to date seriously, or just casually hanging out with someone. For some guys, dating is a way to have fun and meet new people. They're not looking for anything serious and are just enjoying the process of getting to know someone. Other guys might be more interested in finding a serious relationship. They might go on dates with the intention of finding someone they could see themselves being with long-term. And then there are guys who fall somewhere in between. They might not be looking for anything too serious, but they're open to the idea of a relationship if it happens naturally. Dating can be confusing, especially if you are not sure what the other person is looking for. The best way to figure it out is to communicate openly and honestly about your intentions from the beginning. That way, there are no surprises down the road and everyone knows what they're getting into.

What do you call someone you are talking to but not dating?

There's no one definitive answer to this question. It really depends on the situation and the relationship between the two people involved.
